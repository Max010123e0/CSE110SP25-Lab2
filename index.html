New Business

Card UI Component Definition

The team discussed the following definition and characteristics of cards:

  • Cards are contained UI components that group related information
  • Cards often include images, text, and action buttons
  • They provide a clean way to separate different pieces of content

UI Design

Hiroe presented initial UI design concepts:

Utilization of Cards

  • Show info like recipes, articles, profiles, products
  • Use in games (like card games), dashboards, or quizzes
  • Act as links or entry points to deeper content
